Did you know that in B.C., 20% of all lost time injuries are attributed to falls? Training has always been and will continue to be a critical focus in all industries. Our comprehensive one-day end-user program is based on Part 11 of BC regulations and CSA Z259 and Z359 standards. It provides in-depth theory training and hands-on exercises suitable for individuals at any skill level.

Our Fall Protection training program includes

Hazard identification

Relevant regulations

CSA standards

Company/workplace policies

Arrest vs. restraint

Descent/rescue procedures (overview)

Hands-on workshop

System evaluation

Anchor points

Harnesses

Course Materials Provided

Workbook

Supplementary handouts

Wallet card certification (issued upon successful completion)

Important Notices:

While the theory portion of the program takes place in a classroom setting, active participation in the field is essential for successful completion. Participants are required to bring all necessary personal protective equipment (PPE) mandated by their worksite, including:

Steel-toed work boots

High-visibility vest

Hard hat (if required)

Any additional PPE specified by the workplace
