Comprehensive Forklift Training Course

Theory and Practical Components

It comprises two crucial components: a comprehensive theoretical portion and practical hands-on training. This well-rounded approach ensures you receive a complete and well-rounded forklift operator training experience.

Our trainers will guide you through the following key areas

Forklift safety analysis and pre-operation checks: Understanding the necessary safety protocols and conducting thorough inspections before operating the forklift
Job site awareness and hazard analysis: Identifying potential hazards and proactively ensuring a safe working environment
Principles of capacity, stability, and load management: Learning to assess load capacities, maintain stability, and effectively manage loads
Engine, motor, and other lift truck controls: Familiarize yourself with the various controls and their functions within the forklift
Procedures for starting up and shutting down the equipment safely: Mastering the specific protocols for safely starting up and shutting down the forklift you will be authorized to operate
Best practices for steering control and maneuverability: Developing skills in steering control and maneuvering the forklift efficiently and effectively
Refuelling and recharging methods: Understanding proper refuelling and recharging procedures to maintain optimal forklift performance

Once you have acquired sufficient theoretical knowledge, you will move on to the practical hands-on training portion of the course. Under the guidance of a certified course trainer, you will receive valuable hands-on experience operating a forklift.
