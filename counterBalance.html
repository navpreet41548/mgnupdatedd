Elevate Workplace Safety with MGN Forklift Training: Counter Balance

Learn More

Duration: 4 – 6 hours Location: On-site at your location

Training has always been and will continue to be a crucial focus in all industries. Our versatile and comprehensive program offers in-depth instruction on applicable regulations and safe operation of Class 2 narrow aisle forklifts. It includes a combination of theory learning and hands-on exercises, making it suitable for operators at any skill level.

Phase 1 – Theory Training:

Our counterbalance forklift training program includes

Safety protocols: We teach the latest safety guidelines and regulations for forklift operations, enabling participants to navigate hazards and maintain a secure working environment.

Equipment operation: Our program provides comprehensive knowledge of forklift functionality, controls, and safe operation. Participants learn maintenance tasks and identify potential mechanical issues.

Load handling and stability: We cover safe loading/unloading, weight distribution, and strategies for maximizing load stability, preventing accidents.

Maneuvering and spatial awareness: Participants gain expertise in maneuvering forklifts in tight spaces, narrow aisles, and challenging environments. They learn spatial awareness techniques and safe navigation practices

Emergency procedures: We prioritize preparedness, training participants in emergency shutdown, evacuation protocols, and effective communication during critical situations.
